Events

Dribbling Skills: 10am-11am

We have dribbling coaches who will do 1 on 1 and group dribbling drills that will help our youth learn dribbling excersices to improve their overall skills. Coaches will show them videos and give them certain situations and show campers how to best figure out how to manuever throw those situations with dribbling.

Shooting Drills: 11:30am-1pm

College shooting coaches will be in attendance to give campers shooting drills to enhance their shooting. We will work with layups, close-range, mid-range, and even three point shots. No game is complete without free throws, coaches will give tips and best practices to help improve campers shooting precentage.

5 vs 5 Showcase: 2pm-5pm

We will have campers group up into teams and go through 5 on 5 game simulation. This will give campers the chance to use those skills tought throughout the day and give their parents a show. There will be college coaches in attendance and will be able to look over view the upcoming talent.

Recruiter Q&A: 5pm-7pm

College recruiters will take the time to go over what they look for in their future recruiters and give you advise and tips on what to do to get ready for the future. Make sure you take the time to prepare plenty of questions because this will be the time to ask.
